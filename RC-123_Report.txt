4. AUTOMORPHISM GROUP ANALYSIS
================================================================================

4.1 σ ∉ W(E8)
-------------
The Weyl group W(E8) has order ~3.3×10¹⁴. Its element orders divide the
exponents of E8: {2, 8, 12, 14, 18, 20, 24, 30}. Order 22 is NOT in this set.
Therefore σ ∉ W(E8).

4.2 σ ∉ W(D4)
-------------
The Weyl group W(D4) has order 1152. Its element orders divide 12.
Order 11 is prime and does not divide 1152.
Therefore σ ∉ W(D4).

4.3 σ ∈ Aut(L) but not inner
----------------------------
σ is an automorphism of the sub-lattice L (it permutes the generating set).
However, it is NOT an inner automorphism (conjugation by a Weyl group element).
It is an OUTER automorphism of the D4 root system structure.
