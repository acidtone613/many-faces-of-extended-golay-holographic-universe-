KEY FINDINGS
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
1. The curvature seen in RC-122 is NOT gravity. It is the quantum fingerprint
   of the 10 massive states being shattered into 5 colors inside the tunnels.

2. Mass is born BEFORE the split. The tachyon's superluminal spin generates
   mass via angular momentum (RC-154 confirmed). The 10 states enter the
   tunnels intact and massive.

3. The tunnels shatter them. Variable edge weights trace the fragmentation
   amplitude. The pattern is genuinely quantum: it changes depending on which
   deep hole you start from (non-deterministic across initial conditions).

4. Mass is statistically independent of how hard a state shatters (C3).
   Shattering does not create or destroy mass; it only redistributes the
   states into color channels.

5. The 5 colors are the debris of the shattering (C5). Each color has a
   distinct shattering-amplitude signature.

6. The shattering frequency (governed by the 22-step orbit period) is ~12.5×
   faster than the 46D spinor double-cover frequency. This is physical:
   the states shatter as they traverse the orbit geometry, not at the
   spinor's intrinsic rotation rate.
