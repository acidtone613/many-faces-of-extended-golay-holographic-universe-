HONEST LIMITATIONS
================================================================================

1. E6 (1,4) Absence:
   The specific E6 GUT manifold (1,4) with π₁ = Z₁₂ is NOT present in the
   framework as currently constructed. This is a genuine negative result.

   The (1,4) manifold's covering space X^{8,44} is built from dP₆ × dP₆,
   not the 24-cell. The 24-cell CY and X^{8,44} are related by conifold
   transitions in the CY moduli space, but this relationship is not explicit
   in the current framework.

2. Direct Hodge Number Computation:
   My direct lattice point enumeration for the 24-cell in D4 gave inconsistent
   results (28,12) instead of Braun's (20,20). This indicates an error in my
   facet inequality derivation or lattice point counting. I rely on Braun's
   established result rather than my own computation.

3. Decagon (5,22) Self-Mirror Conflict:
   The 24-cell is self-dual, so its CY is self-mirror (20,20). The decagon
   projection gives (5,22), which is not self-mirror. This is consistent
   if the projection breaks the self-duality, but the mechanism is not fully
   understood.
