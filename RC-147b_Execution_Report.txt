In this basis, the physical automorphisms P23, P11, S, and H_L all map
logical_X to logical_X and logical_Z to logical_Z (modulo stabilizers).

This is not a general property of the automorphism group. It is a property
of THIS SPECIFIC BASIS. The systematic basis is an eigenbasis of the group.
