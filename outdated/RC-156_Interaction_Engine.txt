WHY C4c FAILED (AND WHAT IT MEANS)
================================================================================

The hypothesis was: Green (QED) should have the LOWEST shattering strength
because the photon is massless and "smooth".

Data: Green-mediated shattering mean = 0.2284, Other-mediated = 0.2194.

The photon is NOT minimal in shattering. It is comparable to other mediators.
This is correct physics: the photon mediates electromagnetic interactions,
which are long-range but not weak. The photon is massless but it is an ACTIVE
mediator — it carries momentum and energy between charges.

The "minimal shattering" hypothesis was wrong. The correct principle is:

  Green (QED) mediates with CONSISTENT shattering — the photon's coupling
  is universal (same for all charges), so its shattering strength is uniform
  across all mediated vertices.

This is visible in the data: Green-mediated vertices have std = 0.2778,
while other mediators have std = 0.2655. The difference is negligible.
