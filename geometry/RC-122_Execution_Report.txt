================================================================================

icosahedron vertex is NEVER visited.

The missing vertex corresponds to deep hole index: 3 (or its antipode 20).

The orbit's edge structure reveals the tick's action:
  • Short edges (1.05) correspond to adjacent icosahedron vertices
    (edge length of regular icosahedron = ~1.05 in our normalization)
  • Long edges (1.70) correspond to vertices separated by one intermediate vertex

This means the Floquet tick is NOT a simple graph walk. It is a "teleportation"
operator that can jump across the icosahedron in variable steps.
