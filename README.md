### Face Pair 1: Cyclic ⇄ QR (The Engine Faces)

- $\lambda_3 = 3$ (multiplicity 10)

The **Gram gap** $\sqrt{\lambda_1} - \sqrt{\lambda_{12}} = 6$ and **Gram product root** $\sqrt{\lambda_1} \times \sqrt{\lambda_{12}} = 11$ are the framework's single most load-bearing spectral identities.

The duality: cyclic basis has dynamic 23+1 split; QR basis has static 12+12 split. The $\pi$-bridge permutation maps between them.
