delocalized eigenvectors onto a subspace. The eigenvectors have equal

projections do not reproduce the 16/24 factor.

  are isomorphic to O_h ≅ S4 × Z2 (direct product), NOT to 2O ≅ GL(2,3)

  commutes with D."

  representations of 2O (dimensions 2, 2, 2) CANNOT act on 1D spaces.

OBSTRUCTION 4: The eigenvectors are not 2O-covariant
  The eigenvectors are defined with respect to the broken-symmetry
  geometry. 2O does not commute with the Dirac operator, so it does
  not act on the eigenspaces.
