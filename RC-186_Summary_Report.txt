4. TASK 2: 5D DYNAMIC COUPLING (Flow vs Binding)
================================================================================

4.1 PROCEDURE
  • Evolved all 11 Matter deep holes and 13 Dark Matter deep holes through
    22 Floquet ticks, recording their 5-color decagon color at each tick.
  • Computed Mutual Information MI(t) = I(Matter(t); DarkMatter(t)) for
    t = 0..21.
  • Computed numerical derivative dMI/dt = MI(t+1) − MI(t).
  • Evolved all 192 mixed roots through 22 ticks, recording their color
    sequence and 2D projection.
  • Computed color density ρ_c(t) = fraction of roots on color c at tick t.
  • Computed ensemble entropy H(t) = −Σ_c ρ_c(t) log₂ ρ_c(t).
  • Computed Pearson correlation between dρ_c/dt and dMI/dt for each color.
