## 7. ANALYSIS

### 7.1 What Works

1. **Structural constants verified**: α = 308/75 is confirmed as a ratio of Leech lattice dimensions
2. **S-matrix hierarchy correct**: QCD > QED > Weak mixing pattern inherited from RC-159
3. **GIM suppression functional**: Large energy-splitting mixings are correctly dampened
4. **45×45 framework sound**: Diagonal structure from RC-170b/RC-173b is preserved
5. **Photon factory intact**: -9D tunnel produces massless Goldstone mode (RC-151b)

### 7.2 What Fails

1. **Mixing magnitude insufficient**: ΔE_ent = 2.87 bits produces M_mix elements ~0.1-0.2,
   but diagonal elements for heavy fermions are ~1-3. The mixing cannot shift eigenvalues
   enough to bring Down and Charm within 5%.

2. **Compression vs. accuracy tradeoff**: The κ values that give good compression
   (> 3.4×10⁵) do not simultaneously give good accuracy for all 9 fermions.

3. **ΔE_ent computation**: The S-matrix singular value entropy (2.87 bits) may not
   capture the full entanglement in the 144-tick Floquet operator. Computing MI from
   the full U_144 density matrix could yield a larger value.

### 7.3 Root Cause

The formula is **structurally correct** but the **computed ΔE_ent is too small**.

The stripped energy from RC-172 is **171.29 units** (physical energy), while the
computed mutual information is **2.87 bits** (information). The conversion
α · (bits/45) → mixing strength loses a factor of ~60 compared to using the
stripped energy directly.

**Resolution**: Either:
- (a) Recompute ΔE_ent from full U_144 (not projected S^(0)), or
- (b) The 5% target requires RC-177 (dynamic curvature) as noted in RC-175b
