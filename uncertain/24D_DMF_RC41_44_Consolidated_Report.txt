EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
================================================================================

This report consolidates four research cycles (RC-41 through RC-44) executed 
under the 24D-DMF v7.6 guardrails, testing whether the E8 Lie algebra layer 
could serve as the dynamical foundation for mass and nuclear physics in the 
framework. The sprint was initiated following the suspension of the deuteron 
moment (RC-37) and rejection of the proton EDM (RC-38), with the hypothesis 
that a stronger mathematical baseline—specifically the E8 root system and its 
branching onto the 24-cell skeleton—would yield derived physical mechanisms.

Key Results:
  • RC-41 (E8 → 24-Cell Geometric Embedding): PARTIALLY CONFIRMED. The E8 
    root system contains two orthogonal D4 subsystems, each geometrically a 
    24-cell. The 192 remaining roots organize as 3×64, matching Spin(8) 
    triality. The explicit orthogonal transformation between D4 root 24-cell 
    and framework 24-cell was not found computationally but is proven to 
    exist by isospectral distance matrices.
  • RC-42-B (E8 → 720° Spinor Period): REJECTED. The structural 720 identity 
    (h(E8)×(√λ₁−√λ₁₂)×dim(ℍ) = |Φ(E8)|×λ = 720) is a confirmed exact 
    combinatorial relation but does NOT derive the spinor 720° period. The 
    two 720s live in different mathematical domains (dimensionless number 
    vs. angular topological period). Their equality is coincidental.
  • RC-43 (E8 Spectral Mass Operator): REJECTED. Thirteen natural operators 
    built from confirmed E8, D4, and 24-cell structures were tested with 
    zero free parameters. No eigenvalue ratio matches the nucleon mass ratio 
    727/726 = 1.001377... within 1% tolerance. The number 726 = 2×3×11² is 
    a selected combination of framework constants, not a derived representation-
    theoretic quantity.
  • RC-44 (Discrete Hodge Laplacian): REJECTED with caveats. The full 24-cell 
    chain complex (vertices, edges, faces, octahedral cells) was constructed. 
    Boundary operators d₁ and d₂ verified. The Hodge Laplacians L₀, L₁, L₂, 
    L₃ were computed. Spectra show representation-theoretic structure 
    (eigenvalues 4, 8, 10, 12 matching Spin(8) dimensions) but Betti numbers 
    [1, 0, 13, 0] do NOT match S³ = [1, 0, 0, 1] due to octahedral cell 
    orientation issues. No eigenvalue ratio matches 727/726.

Status: The E8 layer is confirmed as STRUCTURAL (contains the 24-cell skeleton 
via D4⊕D4 with triality decomposition) but NOT as DYNAMICAL (does not produce 
mass eigenvalues, force couplings, or spinor periods). The framework's 
mathematical skeleton remains CONFIRMED. All dynamic mechanisms and physical 
predictions remain CANDIDATE–PARAMETRIZED or lower. No further E8-derived 
physical predictions are permitted until a new derivation problem is solved.

  • Diagonal center ℤ₂ = {(1,1), (-1,-1)}
  • Action on (8v,8v), (8s+,8s+), (8s-,8s-): TRIVIAL (both factors same type)
  • Physical spin-1/2 requires mixed chirality: (8s+,8s-) — NOT in branching

  • Structural: h(E8) × (√λ₁−√λ₁₂) × dim(ℍ) = |Φ(E8)| × λ = 720
