2. THE QGP FLOW MODEL
---------------------
192 mixed E8 roots evolve under the Floquet tick operators (P23, P11, H_L).
At each tick, each root projects to 3D and is assigned to its nearest
24-cell face (of 96 triangular faces).

Face occupancy:  occupancy[t][face] = count of roots nearest to face at tick t
QGP distribution: p_face[t] = occupancy[t][face] / 192

================================================================================

3. TASK 1: QGP SUPERPOSITION ENTROPY
--------------------------------------
S_QGP(t) = -Σ p_face[t] · log₂(p_face[t])

Results:
  Range:     [3.6416, 4.9847] bits
  Mean:      4.4897 ± 0.3795 bits
  At Tick 3:  3.9763 bits
  At Tick 11: 4.5338 bits
  Max:        4.9847 bits at Tick 2

Interpretation:
  S_QGP is consistently HIGH (~4.5 bits), indicating the QGP is spread
  across many faces (superposition-like behavior). However, this is
  CLASSICAL Shannon entropy, not quantum von Neumann entropy.

  The QGP occupies 29.3 ± 6.2 faces per tick
  (out of 96), confirming broad but classical distribution.

  Peaks do NOT align with critical ticks (3, 11). The highest entropy
  occurs at Tick 2, not at symmetry breaking or H_L.
