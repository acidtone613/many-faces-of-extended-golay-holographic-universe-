CORE HYPOTHESIS
================================================================================

The S-involution implements a specific, identifiable Clifford gate on the 12
logical qubits that can be decomposed into a finite circuit of:
  - CNOT gates (entangling)
  - Hadamard gates (basis change)
  - S gates (phase π/2)
  - Pauli operators (correction)

The gate is NOT a simple tensor product of single-qubit gates (proven by
C ≠ 0). It is entangling. The exact circuit is the target.

COMPUTATIONAL PROTOCOL
================================================================================

Step 1: Systematic-Form Basis for G₂₄
---------------------------------------
Convert G₂₄ from cyclic form to systematic form [I₁₂ | P] where P is 12×12.
This gives a canonical choice of logical operators aligned with the code
structure. Recompute coset representatives {u_j} and {d_j} in this basis.

Step 2: Recompute Symplectic Matrix M in Systematic Basis
----------------------------------------------------------
Recompute A, B, C, D blocks with the systematic-form coset representatives.
Verify B = 0, A invertible, C ≠ 0, M² = I are basis-independent.

Step 3: Decompose M into Clifford Circuit
------------------------------------------
Use standard symplectic decomposition algorithm:
  - Row-reduce A over F₂ to identify CNOT structure
  - Decompose C block to identify entangling component
  - Extract phase information (S gates, Pauli corrections)
  - Count CNOT, H, S gates

Target: circuit with ≤ 50 two-qubit gates.

Step 4: Verify by Direct Simulation
------------------------------------
Apply the decomposed circuit to the 12 logical basis states |0⟩^⊗12, |1⟩^⊗12,
and superposition states. Verify the action matches S's conjugation on Paulis.

Step 5: A/B Orbit Correlation Test
-----------------------------------
The engine's two 11-element orbits A and B are swapped by S in the coordinate
representation. Test whether the logical gate has a block structure reflecting
this swap (e.g., a 6+6 qubit partition with cross-CNOTs between blocks).
