All 6 falsification criteria PASS. Hypothesis H1 (period 46) FAILS. Hypothesis H2
(large logical group) PASSES. Hypothesis H3 (wave structure) is CONDITIONAL.

8. HYPOTHESIS ASSESSMENT
================================================================================

H1 (Primary): Period-46 logical dynamics
─────────────────────────────────────────
Expected: 46 (= 2 × 23)
Actual:   12 (state return) / 144 (full operator)

ANALYSIS: The period 46 was derived from the assumption that H_L and P23
generate a dihedral group D23 of order 46. However:

1. P23 and P11 do NOT commute (neither physically nor logically)
2. The dihedral relation S·P23·S = P23^{-1} does NOT hold in the logical basis
3. The combined Floquet operator with P23, P11, and H_L has a more complex
   period structure governed by LCM(23,11) = 253 and the H_L schedule

The actual state return at tick 12 arises from the specific cancellation:
After 11 ticks of engine evolution (t=1 to 11) plus the H_L at t=0 and t=11,
the accumulated symplectic matrix has B=0. This is a non-trivial cancellation
that depends on both P23 and P11.

VERDICT: FAIL — The dynamics is periodic and non-trivial, but the period is
12, not 46. The underlying physical intuition (automorphisms drive logical
evolution) is correct, but the specific period prediction was wrong.

H2 (Secondary): Large logical group
────────────────────────────────────
⟨P23, P11⟩ has 253 elements (the full engine G = C23 ⋊ C11)
⟨P23, S⟩ has 6072 elements (much larger than D23)

VERDICT: PASS — The group generated by the automorphisms on the logical
space is significantly larger than D23. It contains the full non-abelian
engine and additional structure from S.

H3 (Tertiary): Discrete wave with interference
──────────────────────────────────────────────
H1 fails, so the period-46 wave is not observed. However, the trajectory
exhibits a different wave-like structure: a periodic sawtooth oscillation
between basis state and full superposition.

VERDICT: CONDITIONAL — A wave structure exists, but it has period 144
(full operator) / 12 (state return), not 46. The interference pattern
is maximal (4096 amplitudes) during superposition phases.
