3.2 CRITICAL FINDINGS
---------------------

FINDING 1: Wilson Loop Triviality
  The spatial Wilson loop has zero phase. This means the 3D icosahedron
  rotation induced by the Floquet tick is a real orthogonal transformation
  with determinant +1. The eigenvalues are (1, e^{+/-i*theta}), and their
  product is real and positive.

  IMPLICATION: CP-violating phase (if any) must come from the TEMPORAL
  holonomy — the evolution of tunnel capacities over time — not from
  spatial geometry.

FINDING 2: Off-Diagonal Suppression
  The 0.001 coefficient on M_color suppresses the eigenvalue spread.
  The inter-fermion coupling (inter_scale = 1e-5) is even weaker.

  IMPLICATION: The 45x45 operator is effectively block-diagonal. The
  mass hierarchy comes almost entirely from the diagonal y-factors,
  not from mixing. This is why curvature corrections (which only affect
  the diagonal) cannot fix the systematic residuals.

FINDING 3: Energy Level Inversion
  With kappa < 0, larger base energies produce smaller masses (because
  exp(kappa*E) is decreasing). With kappa > 0, larger energies produce
  larger masses.

  The optimal kappa is around +1.15 to +1.20, meaning the mass ordering
  follows the energy ordering. But the energy levels themselves are
  fixed by the 22D Hamiltonian H0 and cannot be arbitrarily adjusted
  without breaking the geometric structure.

FINDING 4: Generation Structure Mismatch
  The generation grouping in the framework (gen 0 = leptons, gen 1 = light
  quarks, gen 2 = heavy quarks) does not match the mass ordering within
  generations. For example:
    - Gen 0: electron (0.5 MeV) << muon (105 MeV) << tau (1.8 GeV)
    - Gen 1: up (2 MeV) ~ down (5 MeV) << strange (95 MeV)
    - Gen 2: charm (1.3 GeV) << bottom (4.2 GeV) << top (173 GeV)

  The 3-fold generation structure is too coarse. There are actually
  9 distinct mass scales, not 3.

3.3 LIMITATIONS
---------------

  1. CURVATURE WEIGHTS ARE PHENOMENOLOGICAL
     The weights (w_tick, w_shatter, w_wilson) are fitted, not derived
     from first principles. A geometric derivation would require computing
     the moduli-space velocity of CICY manifolds under Floquet evolution.

  2. WILSON LOOP IS SPATIALLY TRIVIAL
     The zero Wilson phase means the spatial holonomy carries no
     topological information. The relevant holonomy may be temporal.

  3. OFF-DIAGONAL MIXING IS TOO WEAK
     The 0.001 coefficient makes the operator nearly diagonal. Generation
     mixing (RC-174) and tunnel operators (RC-171) are needed but were
     not combined with curvature in this cycle.

  4. ENERGY CANNOT DISAPPEAR
     The framework strips energy in tunnels but does not model where
     that energy goes. The "shattering" amplitudes measure edge-length
     fluctuations but do not conserve energy.
